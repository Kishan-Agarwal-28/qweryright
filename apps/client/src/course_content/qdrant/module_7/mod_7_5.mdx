## The Qdrant Cheat Sheet

| Action           | API / Logic                | Purpose                                    |
| :--------------- | :------------------------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| **Store**        | `UPSERT`                   | Add vectors and metadata.                  |
| **Search**       | `SEARCH`                   | Find the "Nearest Neighbors" (meaning).    |
| **Filter**       | `payload_index` + `FILTER` | Narrow down by facts (JSON).               |
| **Analyze**      | `COSINE` / `EUCLIDEAN`     | Choose the right math for similarity.      |
| **Speed**        | `HNSW`                     | The secret to searching millions of items. |
| **Intelligence** | `RAG`                      | Connect Qdrant to LLMs for smart answers.  |

---

## Final Words of Wisdom

The world of Vector Databases is moving fast.

- Use **Qdrant** when you need high-performance search in production.
- Use **Embeddings** to capture the "Nuance" of human language.
- Use **Payloads** to keep your data structured and grounded.
