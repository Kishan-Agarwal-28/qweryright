# Module 6 - Summary

This was our "Deep Dive" into search quality.

---

## What we covered:

- **Highlighting:** Showing the user exactly where their search term matched.
- **Advanced Boosting:** Prioritizing fields like `title` or `isSponsored`.
- **Custom Scoring:** Using BM25 and decay functions to handle popularity and recency.
- **Pipelines:** Using normalization and medical/brand expansions to make search smarter.

---

## Key Takeaway

Search isn't just a technical challenge; it's a **UX challenge**. Highlighting, boosting, and scoring are tools that help you align your search engine's results with your users' expectations.
