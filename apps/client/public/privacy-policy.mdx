## 1. What Information I Collect & Why

I only collect the absolute minimum required to make the application function.

### A. Account Data (If you sign up)

You can explore parts of the site without an account. If you choose to sign up to save your progress, I store:

- **Identity:** Your email address, username, and profile picture (from GitHub/Google).
- **Authentication:** I use **Better Auth**. I do not see or store your actual passwords. I only store the secure tokens required to keep you logged in.

### B. Your Learning Progress

To provide the "gamified" experience (streaks, completion tracking), I save:

- Which lessons you have completed.
- Your submitted solutions (code).
- Your current streak and learning stats.

### C. Technical Logs

Like almost every website on the internet, the hosting provider (e.g., Vercel/Netlify) and database provider may automatically log standard technical data when you visit:

- Your IP address (for security and DDoS protection).
- User Agent (browser type, to fix bugs).

## 2. The "Local-First" Architecture

This app is unique because it runs heavy technologies directly in your browser. This is great for your privacy.

- **PGLite & Mongo (In-Browser Databases):** When you run practice queries, they are often executing inside a database running _inside your browser tab_. This practice data does not constantly stream to my server. It lives in your device's memory/cache.
- **Local AI (WebLLM):** If you use the "Local AI" features, the model downloads to your browser cache. Your prompts and code discussions with this local bot are processed on your deviceâ€™s GPU/CPU and are **not** sent to me or any cloud provider.

## 4. How I Use AI

The platform offers two types of AI. It is important to know the difference:

1. **Cloud AI (e.g., Gemini/OpenAI):** If you ask for a "Deep Explanation" or "Query Optimization," I must send your code snippet to the cloud provider to get an answer.
2. **Local AI (WebLLM):** If you select the in-browser model, your code **never leaves your device**. It is processed entirely by your hardware.

**I do not use your code submissions to train my own AI models.**
